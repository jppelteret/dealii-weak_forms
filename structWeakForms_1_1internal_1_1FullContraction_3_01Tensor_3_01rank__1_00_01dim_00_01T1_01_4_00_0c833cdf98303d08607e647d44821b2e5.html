Contraction with a tensor

Here we recognise that the shape functions can only be scalar valued (dealt with in the above specializations), vector valued (Tensors of rank 1), rank-2 tensor valued or rank-2 symmetric tensor valued. For the rank 1 and rank 2 case, we already have full contraction operations that we can leverage.
